The Lounge

Unwind in the Lounge, a comfortable and stylish space perfect for relaxing and spending quality time with your pets. Personalize it to make it your ultimate cozy retreat.

The Bedroom

Retreat to the Bedroom, a serene and inviting sanctuary for rest and relaxation. Unlock the bedroom and watch as your lights illuminate the dark of the night as you sleep.

The Nursery

Welcome new beginnings in the Nursery, a space designed for your bundles of joy. Once unlocked, you can name your baby and watch them sleep, play, or get carried around by your character and partner.

The Kitchen

Step into the Kithen and get your cooking gloves on. Prepare delightful meals and snacks using ingredients from your farm for your home, as well as your partner's bento box for work.

The Bathroom

Enter your Bathroom to rest in a tranquil atmosphere where you can unwind after a long day. Add personal touches such as potted plants or framed artwork to enhance your bathroom's vibes.

The Garden

Enjoy the quiet breeze of the Garden, a serene spot to play with your furry friends. Witness the seasons change and watch different animals enjoy your garden.

The Farm

Immerse yourself in your very own Farm, a picturesque countryside escape where you can embrace the joys of rural life. Tend to your crops and care for adorable farm animals to unlock recipes in the Kitchen.
